The Disastrous Life of Saiki K.

Everyday is a challenge for an extraordinary teenage boy named Saiki Kusuo, who was born with psychic abilities that are more of an annoyance than a gift. He tries very hard to hide these powers because he wants to live a normal life. However, living with these powers and freakish people around him makes it hard for him to achieve his desires.
